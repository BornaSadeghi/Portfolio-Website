Project 2: Get a Grip

Me and my team design a system for securely transferring a surgical instrument to an autoclave for sterilization.

Engineering Scenario

One of the most important tasks in surgery includes sterilization of the equipment used in order to remove microbes, viruses. Sterilization is commonly performed by autoclaves; a process in which instruments are placed in a container that allows exposure to pressurized steam.
