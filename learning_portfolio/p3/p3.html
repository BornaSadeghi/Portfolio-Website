Project 3: Automated Recycling Station

A recycling station system that classifies between different types of containers and carries them to their appropriate bins.

Engineering Scenario

Only about 70% of items placed in recycling bins are actually recyclable, and in Canada, for plastics that are recyclable, only about 9% of them are actually recycled instead of being incinerated or placed in a landfill.
